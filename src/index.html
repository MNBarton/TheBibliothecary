Would you like to order a book?

The process is simple! Just follow these steps

  1. Call the Bibliothecary. We are open every third Tuesday from 1 - 2p
  2. Pay for the book with a dragon's tooth or cat fur
  3. Wait for your book to arrive. Processing time may be up to 1 year
  4. If you decide you do not want the book, keep it. A Cautionary Note: Abandoned books are particulary vengeful and The Bibliothecary is not responsible for any damageds incurred to self or property
